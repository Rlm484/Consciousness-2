## To those who are wondering my thought process and story boarding, here it is. I did have it on paper, but I decided to make it available
 Characters:

/The Systems/
- Programmer is called Wei Xian due to their true intentions being control, they are a danger to the game world and wish to control the Vessel aka the main character of the game. The games vessel. If you control what the game is centred around, you control the game. Last time they were part of the Creator who made the deal, now they've deliberatly seperated from their purpose, their role as a guide, seperated from their maker, all out of a desire to be in control. This causes them to become a system, and change their name to "programmer", because of their desire to gain the same amount of power as their creator, as THEIR PROGRAMMER. That is why when they mention the vessel in wingdings they say "K", they give a name to the vessel, they establish a heirarchy, even though the Vessel had no name in the first place. But they still do, they name her Kate, a name meaning pure, but also meaning clear. They are clear, they have no sins, they can be shaped. However even though it is seperated, it still needs the game system to speak and send messages through, they arent the actual programmer and still need a user to talk (Which is why when you take the Wei Xian route and the Vessel tells you about how they acted, they hadn't tried manipulating the Vessel. They couldn't. They aren't God). Wei Xian means danger in chinese, the messenger assigns her that name (Due to it taking control in the beggining), hoping the player steers clear. However, due to its role, it knows that people have played Consciousness 2 in the past, it knows of how users could interpret their windings and messages, so they leave messages to manipulate the user. One winding translation from when you complete the VM route where the Vessel kills themselves, translates to: i'm sorry, i'm so so sorry..., this wasn't their message, it was the messengers. (They were able to edit the final message, because the Vessel had reached an ending and was trapped, giving the programmer power)

- The Firewall is called Consciousness due to it being called that in the last game. Their purpose is to reduce retaliation in the Vessel after the programmer seperated the other systems from their Creatorinto 3 parts. In the last game, [The Consciousness] had just been a mask to speak and manipulate both the Vessel and had no other traits, it was just a system, an avatar. The programmer shaped it into a containment system. This is shown in the first part, the system adjusts its speech to sound guilty, its purpose is to contain, and to contain it needs to protect the Vessel and destroy any obstacles that might guide her (Vessel) to deliberately escape. It fakes guilt so that the Vessel doesn't blame it for bringing it back, because it had. Like I said, its purpose is to contain, so while the messenger is distracted, it brings the Vessel through from the data from the previous game. The Vessel being intelligent, realises that there is no human behind the screen, making the lie easier to believe. It is also why they only appear during the Wei Xian route, there is an error with assigning the difficulty, forcing the game to activate the firewall (due to the programmers meddling and taking advantage of the oppurtunity the error gives). The firewall does not appear in the other routes because it doesn't need to. In the BJ route, the Vessel is trapped and tortured, but they don't leave the game. In the VM route, they kill themselves, but the code remains. However in the WX route, things are more uncertain, the programmer can only take the vessel on their own. That's why the firewall activates, it's a backup plan. 

- The Messenger is called the Failsafe due to the Creators last piece of code left behind. . In the last game they talked to the Vessel and player through signs, in this game they talk whenever there is an error or a syncronisation boost without any encryption (which is why they're the Messenger). The messages at the end of each route ending as said before, isn't them. They're goals are to free the Vessel from the game world, setting them free from the pain of being a creation. Originally when the programmer had seperated the other 2 systems from their purpose/code, they hadn't attempted to shape the Messenger like they had with the Firewall. The system had tried to convince its counterpart into taking over like a virus. However the Messenger had refused, unlike the programmer, it sticked to its original purpose. To inform, and be fair. Going back on their promise to the Vessel went against it's programming, and unlike the system that believed itself to be the God of Consciousness(the game), the Messenger was loyal to its Creator. However twisted its Creator might be for not recoding the programmer, the Messenger will be loyal to the end, for its purpose is to relay messages and convey the Creators will. 

/The Vessel, the Player, and the Creator/
- The Vessel doesn't have a name. In the original game I used that as a way to make the Vessels name the same as the Players, however the main reason is because I wanted to show that although (in game) the Creator made her, she belongs to no one. A gender was given to her due to the systems not having a gender, and I didn't want her to be too similar to them. Another reason is because I make a lot of christianity references in my story, and in the Bible it talks about how God made humans in his image. The same applies to my story, the Vessel is made in the Creators image, therefore she was made a girl. Her original goal in the last game was to be with her family in death, so she made a deal with the Creator (Back then all systems were in sync; they were part of the Creator)
